Theme Neon

Responsive Multipurpose Premium Odoo Theme

RTL Supported

Theme Neon is a fully responsive Odoo eCommerce theme with a clean, crafted, organized and elegant design for all online stores. Neon Odoo theme is a multipurpose, dynamic theme that fits any eCommerce industry such as Fashion, Furniture, Mobile, Bicycle, Jewellery, Corporate, etc...

Neon Odoo theme is easy to customize, highly flexible with a powerful Visual Snippet Builder that helps to build a fully functional dynamic online store. The theme offers a powerful set of features that add usability and style to any website. Neon helps any store owner to make their business highly effective and improve revenue in the short run. You can update anytime your Odoo store appearance by installing a theme with a few easy steps.

List of Features

  • Theme Color Chooser

    Take control without coding to change the theme color. Choose your website branding color anytime from color-chooser easily.

  • Box/Full Layout Options

    On click update layout option. Choose Full width or Box layout for overall website look & feel.

  • 6 Fonts Combinations

    Integration with Google Fonts. Best preset 6 fonts options. Select different heading and body fonts.

  • Background Color and Images Options

    Update your website with preset background color or Image for Box layout option.

  • 11 Header Options

    Latest trendy eleven elegant dynamic headers layout available for your website and one-click update.

  • 11 Footer Options

    The best combination of good looking and functional eleven pre-defined footer options collection for your store.

  • Footer Dark, Light and Gray Color Options

    Nice and improved Dark, Light and Gray color footer options for your website layout. More Details

  • Custom Breadcrumb

    Powerful and editable tool for showing breadcrumb on your page with a professional look.

  • Blog slider Snippet

    Fully customizable, very useful and effective blog post slider. Easy to configure and user friendly.

  • Brand Page

    Auto-build your A to Z list of all your brands on a single page. Enhance your brand presentation on the store.

  • Brand slider

    Assist your customers to identify which brands promoted by store. You can add a slider anywhere using the slider snippet.

  • Dynamic Mega Menu

    Guide your customers to discover products and collections to make them stay with an attractive fully dynamic menu.

  • Dynamic Product Snippets

    Create your product sliders for promoting products. Fully configurable and dynamic sliders with a lot of options.

  • Shop Toggle, Full-Width Layout, and Box Layout Options

    New and latest trendy three different style shop layout that you can choose for your store: Full Width, Toggle and Box. More Details

  • 2 Product Box Style

    Two different product layout on the shop page with Add to cart, Quick view, Wishlist and Compare. More Details

  • Snippet Builder

    Unique and most amazing feature, 100+ snippets that helps you to build your website with single click. More Details

  • Header Quick Search

    Fastest alternative to regular product page search. Expanded quick search bar in the all headers.

  • Vertical/Horizontal Product multi-image options

    Let's choose between a horizontal and vertical slider for multiple product images on the product page. More Details

  • Product Details Basic Tabs

    Add tabs to product detail page for description, alternative products, reviews, attribute table and accessory products.

    Image popup slider is responsive and providing the best user experience to check all multiple images in a big popup slider.

  • Product Image Zoom

    Image zoom effect on the hover of a product to view all the parts of product.
